Granny Square

Need To Know Stiches

  • Double Crochet
  • Slip Stich
  • Magic ring
  • Chaining

Ch5 sl st furthest chain from the hook. (Now you have your loop.)

R1.Ch3 (chain counts as a first double crochet). Now, into the loop work (2Dc, ch2, 3Dc), next work into the loop (3Dc, ch2, 3Dc), repeat step two more times. Sl st into the top of ch3.

R2.Ch3. In the space made by the ch2 –-the chain 2 space-– work (2Dc, ch2, 3Dc, ch1). Then in the next ch2 space work, (3Dc, ch2, 3Dc, ch1) repeat step two more times. Sl st into the top of ch3.

R3.Ch3. In the first ch2 space work (2Dc, ch2, 3Dc, ch1). Now into the ch1 space –same as ch2 space but ch1– work [(3Dc, ch1), then next ch2 space work (3Dc, ch2, 3Dc, ch1)] repeat that two more times. Sl st into the top of ch3

R4.Ch3. in the first ch2 space (2Dc, ch2, 3Dc, ch1). Now, in your ch1 space work [(3Dc, ch1), in the next ch1 space work (3Dc, ch1), and then in the next ch2 space work (3Dc, ch2, 3Dc, ch1)] repeat this two more times. Sl st into the top of ch3. Repeat until the desired size; fasten off. Weave in ends.
